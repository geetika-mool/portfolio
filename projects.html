Project 3 – Smartphone SoC Product Validation

Objective:

Drive end‑to‑end validation and release management for smartphone SoC platforms, ensuring quality delivery to OEM customers.

Highlights:

Led product validation across OS bring‑up and feature releases. Translated customer requirements into acceptance criteria and test plans. Partnered cross‑functionally with hardware, software, and DevOps teams to align execution with roadmap priorities. Established QA process management and release reporting workflows via TestRail. Improved release visibility by 90%, reduced reporting cycle time from 24+ hours to under 5 minutes, cut release time by 50%, and enhanced stakeholder satisfaction through reliable, timely product delivery.
